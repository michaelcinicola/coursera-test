And the proper use of this next one is not for adding extra spaces (whoopsie!) but for keeping a phrase like this together and not allowing it to break during the resize of an element or a browser.
And finally, using the quote entitiy will really make sure previous, less robust character sets, often found in html emails, will still display "quotes" instead of odd characters as a replacement. They may not look as good as the curly ones, but they are more reliable to appear.
